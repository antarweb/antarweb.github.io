About Me

I am Antar, a recent graduate in Zoology from the National University, driven by a passion for merging science and art. My academic journey has provided me with a deep understanding of animal biology, behavior, and conservation, while my professional experiences have allowed me to explore my creative side through graphic design. During my six months as a Graphic Designer at Farhana's Brainstation, I honed my skills in Photoshop. This role enabled me to create visually captivating contents. I collaborated closely with a talented team to bring innovative ideas to life, enhancing my ability to integrate scientific concepts with artistic expression. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and I have experience in making design. My unique combination of zoological expertise and creative design skills allows me to produce educational and engaging content that stands out. This portfolio is a showcase of my journey, skills, and achievements. Here, you will find a selection of my work that highlights my versatility as a Graphics Designer and my commitment to excellence. I am excited to collaborate with like-minded professionals and organizations that value creativity and innovation.
Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
